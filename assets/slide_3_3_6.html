Phase II study in refractory aplastic anaemia: common adverse events
  • Investigator deemed that 30 patients (70%) had at least one AE related to treatment
  • Nausea, fatigue, cough, diarrhoea and headache were the most common events considered related to eltrombopag treatment, occurring in ≥20% of patients
  • The AEs observed reflect the well-known safety profile of eltrombopag and events expected in this patient population
