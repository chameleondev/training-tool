HOW TO USE

This integrated training programme is designed for the training purposes of the Revolade/Promacta team. The modular content within this tool has been developed for this purpose and follows with a self-learning assessment.

The following information explains how you use this tool and navigate within the content. This page can be accessed at any point from the Homepage.

The content within this tool is divided into two themes:

  • Content within the red theme provides basic information that should be fully understood by all employees undergoing the training
  • Content within the blue theme provides additional detailed information for employees who require more in-depth information on specific topics
