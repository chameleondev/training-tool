Lymphopoiesis
  • Antigen-independent lymphopoiesis
    • Not driven by antigen exposure
    • Occurs in the primary lymphoid organs
      • Thymus: T-lymphocytes
      • Fetal liver/adult bone marrow: B-lymphocytes
  • Antigen-dependent lymphopoiesis
    • Naïve T cell or B-cell is exposed to an antigen
    • This results in the formation of cellular clones, are capable of specifically targeting the antigen
    • Occurs in secondary lymphoid tissues (e.g. in the spleen and lymph nodes)
