Management of refractory aplastic anaemia: the UK experience
  • Physicians should consider referring patients with refractory aplastic anaemia to a specialist centre (with expertise in management of the disease) to:
    • Reassess the patient to confirm the diagnosis of aplastic anaemia
    • Gain access to advanced molecular testing techniques that may assist diagnoses of exclusion
    • Provide patients with the opportunity to discuss the pros and cons of different treatment options available for refractory aplastic anaemia
  • Most treatment options for refractory aplastic anaemia are considered experimental, so enrolment into a clinical trial should also be considered
