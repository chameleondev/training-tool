The impact of T-cell-mediated destruction of the bone marrow: haematopoietic failure
  • Immune attack of HSCs and progenitor cells results in their apoptosis and haematopoietic failure2
  • This results in pancytopenia and bone marrow hypoplasia2
    • Causes a significant reduction in the numbers of blood cell types
