Classification of aplastic anaemia
Classification Criteria
Non-severe (moderate) Hypocellular bone marrow with peripheral blood values do not meet the criteria for severe or very severe aplastic anaemia
Severe

Bone marrow cellularity equal to 25% of the normal control, or 25–50% with <30% residual HSCs

AND two out of three of the following peripheral blood criteria:

  • Neutrophil count: <0.5×109/l
  • Platelet count: <20×109/l
  • Reticulocyte count: <20×109/l
Very severe Severe aplastic anaemia with neutrophil count <0.2×109/l
