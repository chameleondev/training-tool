Innate immunity
  • Innate immunity
    • First line of defence against foreign bodies
    • Non-specific immune response
  • Main components
    • Cells (e.g. macrophages, NK cells and granulocytes)
    • Soluble mediators (e.g. complement and cytokines)
  • Cellular elements engulf foreign material and/or release digestive enzymes to destroy it
    • Myeloid cells mainly degrade and remove damaged cells
  • Soluble elements recruit phagocytic cells to the site of infection/inflammation
