Thrombocytes (platelets)
  • Second most abundant cell type in the blood
    • ~150–440×109 cells/l
  • Cell fragments with granular cytoplasm but no nucleus
  • Derived from megakaryocyte (MK) cells in the bone marrow
  • Vital role in facilitating blood clotting after injury to blood vessels
    • Granules contain substances required during platelet activation and promotion of aggregation
  • Circulation lifespan is 10–12 days
