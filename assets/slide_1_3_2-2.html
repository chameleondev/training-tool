Myelopoiesis
  • Myelopoiesis is regulated by the combined actions of several haematopoietic growth factors
    • IL-3 and granulocyte macrophage CSF (GM-CSF) act together on myeloid stem cells (colony-forming unit granulocyte, erythrocyte, monocyte, megakaryocyte; CFU-GEMM)
    • The action of different growth factors on the common progenitor (CFU-GM) results in the production of the different cell types
      • Granulocyte CSF (G-CSF): neutrophils
      • Macrophage CSF (M-CSF): monocytes
      • IL-5: eosinophils
      • IL-3: basophils
